Skilled Independent Visa (SubClass-189)

For talented individuals with significant credentials and expertise that Australia’s labour market demands, there is a subclass 189 visa. With this visa, you are not dependent on any employer, state, or territory for sponsorship in order to live and work anywhere in Australia for an extended period of time. The Government of Australia invites skilled professionals to enter Australia on a permanent residence visa for 5 years and obtain citizenship after staying for 4 years.

To be able to submit a valid visa application, the applicant needs to first submit an Expression of Interest through Skill Select. This can be done onshore or offshore.

Skilled Nominated Visa (Subclass 190)

If you are a skilled worker? If so, you may be able to proceed with the subclass 190 visa. You must be nominated by a state or territory government in order to apply for this visa, which allows you to reside and work in that state or territory. This visa is also a permanent residence visa granted for 5 years and an applicant can apply for citizenship after 2 years of stay in the particular state that sponsors your application.
