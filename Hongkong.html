HONG KONG IMMIGRATION CONSULTANT

Hong Kong Immigration Program Is Called, Quality Migrant Admission Scheme (QMAS). This Is A Quota-Based System. This Program Is Intended To Attract Skilled Individuals & Professionals From Different Fields Of Work Who Wish To Settle In Hong Kong For The Purpose Of Employment Or Start A New Business On Their Own. The QMAS Visa Is Granted Initially For A Period Of One Year.

Eligibility: The Applicant Has To Satisfy Certain Pre-Requisites For Quality Migrant Admission Scheme. There Are Two Options To Meet The Points Test In Either General Or Achievement Based Points Test. The Applicant Has To Receive Either A General Or Achievement Based Points Test.The applicant has to receive in-Principal approval letter for an Interview in HK. The main applicant has to attend an Interview along with the all the family members included in the application during Visa filing with their original documents.

Requirements For The Hong Kong Quality Migrant Admission Scheme Program Applicants Who Are Interested & Eager To Move To Hong Kong As A Job Seeker, Have To Fulfil The Pre Requisite:

  1. Applicant Should Be Minimum At-Least 18 Years Of Age
  2. Must Hold A Bachelor Or Master’s Degree With The Highest Percentage
  3. The Applicant Must Hold The Good Ability In Communication And Have Proficient English Language Skills. The Suitable And Average Score In IELTS Is 6.0 For The Hong Kong QMAS.
  4. Provide Evidence Of Clean Character And Not Have Been A Part Of Any Unlawful Activities And Submit Their Past Police Clearance Certificates From The Countries, They Have Lived In For Last 10 Months In The Past 10 Years.
  5. Sufficient Funds Statement To Support Themselves And Family In Hong Kong During Their Stay Covering All Their Expenses Including Housing Daily Living Cost, Transportation The Applicant Itself.
  6. The Hong Kong QMAS Is A Points-Based Migration Program. Applicant Must Be Able To Score A Minimum Of 80 Points Under The General Points Test And A Maximum Of 225 Points Under The Achievement Points Test

There Is Also An Achievement-Based Points Test Factor For Individuals With Exceptional Talent Or Skill Who Have Outstanding Achievements Like Having Received An Award Of Exceptional Achievement For Example – Olympic Medals, Nobel Prize, National / International Awards Or Can Prove That His Or Her Work Has Been Acknowledged By His/Her Peers Or Contributed Notably To The Development Of His/Her Field.
